Administrators
==============

The preferred mode of contact is via the KnowledgeLab slack channel #cloudkotta. If you do not have
access to this channel please email the admins:

* Yadu Nand B <yadu@uchicago.edu>
* Eamon Duede <eduedue@uchicago.edu>

Here are some actions the require prior approval:

* Adding a new user.
* Privileges to access protected datasets (S3 and RDS)
* Moving protected datasets to S3 and/or RDS database.
